Privacy

The Shadow AI Scanner demo runs entirely in your browser. When you select a folder or files, your browser reads those files locally to perform pattern matching.

What we do

  • • Scan text locally for common indicators (keys, AI libraries, prompts)
  • • Mask detected secrets in outputs
  • • Allow exporting reports as Markdown or JSON (downloaded locally)

What we do NOT do

  • • No uploading of files to a server
  • • No storage of your selected files by this site
  • • No analytics cookies required for the demo
