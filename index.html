FAQ

Common questions

Are the keys real?
No. The demo dataset contains fake example keys. In real scans, detected secrets are always masked in outputs.
Do you upload our files?
No. This web demo reads files locally in your browser. For enterprise deployments, we can run everything inside your environment.
Is this a full security audit?
It’s an indicator-based first pass: fast detection + evidence. It can have false positives and is meant to accelerate deeper remediation work.
What’s the business impact?
Fewer incidents, faster audits, and less vendor/tool sprawl. You reduce accidental leakage risk while keeping AI productivity high.
